1. Pointed וְלָרֻאוּבֵנִ֨י (qubuts under the resh) in Jacobson. In my opinion this is excessive adherence to ל because ל is anomalous here. No reader-friendly edition will be pointed that way.

2. חֹ֥דֶשׁ seems like a weird choice since a short not long o would be expected. Seems like a word with qamats qatan would have been a better choice. Maybe קׇדְשִֽׁי׃ or similar?

3. Pointed שֶׁשֱּׁזָפַ֖תְנִי (ḥataf segol under the second shin) in Jacobson. In my opinion this is excessive adherence to ל because ל is anomalous here. No reader-friendly edition will be pointed that way.
